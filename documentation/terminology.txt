KSK
    Key Signing Key
    KSK is used to sign the DNSKEY RRset at the zone apex.

ZSK
    Zone Signing Key
    ZSK is used to sign zone all content (no needed to
    sign DNSKEY RRset also)

USB FD
    Universal Serial Bus Flash Drive.
    Used as removable storage. No computers uses other storage except RAM.

TEB
    Tamper Evident Bag.
    Used to store items such that later examination can determine if
    someone may have had physical access to the contents. TEBs have unique
    serial numbers.

KC
    Key Ceremony
    Key ceremonies are tightly controlled events where keys are generated.
    It's also the only place where the KSK is ever constructed, reconstructed
    and used.
